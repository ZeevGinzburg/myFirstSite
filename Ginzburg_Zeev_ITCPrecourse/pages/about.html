About me

Hi!

My name is Zeev Ginzburg. I'm 38 years old, married to Tama, and the father of Maayan, Bezalel, Zamir, Adi, Libby and Neta. I live in Pardes Hanna, and work as a consultant in a company called "Pareto Group".

Why Coding?

My interest in programming stems from the intellectual challenge in the field, the ability to create new things, benefit society by creating technological solutions to various problems, and the wish to upgrade my income ... I am in the beginning, and hope to soon find the right place for me in this whole space.

Short Resume

For the past five years I have been working as an economic-strategic consultant at "Pareto group". I participated in various projects, mainly those related to the public sector, for example: projects related to the cost of living for the Ministry of Economy, projects in the field of recycling and waste for the Ministry of Environmental Protection, Monitoring of Israel Railways' development activities, and more.

I have a bachelor's degree in philosophy, economics and political science from the Hebrew University, and a master's degree in philosophy - also from the Hebrew University.

As mentioned, my knowledge of programming is pretty basic - I started a few online introductory courses ("Coursera", "Udemy", "Edx"). I hope that my studies at ITC will lead to a leap forward in my professional level..
